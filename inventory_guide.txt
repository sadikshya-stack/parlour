BEAUTY PARLOUR INVENTORY MANAGEMENT GUIDE
==========================================

HOW TO ADD NEW ITEMS TO INVENTORY:

1. Open inventory.html in your browser
2. Click the "Add New Item" button (blue button with + icon)
3. Fill in the form:
   - SKU: Unique code for the item (e.g., HC006 for Hair Care item 6)
   - Item Name: Name of the product
   - Category: Choose from:
     • Hair Care
     • Skincare
     • Makeup
     • Nail Care
     • Tools & Equipment
     • Cleaning Supplies
   - Supplier: Name of the supplier (optional)
   - Quantity: Current stock quantity
   - Unit Price: Price per item
   - Reorder Level: Minimum quantity before reordering
   - Description: Brief description of the item
4. Click "Save Item" to add to inventory
